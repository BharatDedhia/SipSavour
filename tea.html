Masala Elaichi Chai

Masala elaichi chai, also known as spiced cardamom tea, is a popular and aromatic variation of traditional chai in India. It is a delightful blend of black tea, milk, and a combination of warm spices, with cardamom being the primary flavoring agent. This tea is cherished for its rich, fragrant taste and soothing qualities, making it a favorite beverage among chai enthusiasts.

Ingredients:

Tea powder, milk, cardamom, cinnamon, elaichi, ginger, sugar.

Black Tea

Black tea is a type of tea that is more oxidized than green, white, and oolong teas. It is made from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant, just like all other true teas. The key difference is the degree of oxidation, which results in the dark, robust, and full-bodied flavor of black tea.

Yellow Tea

Yellow tea is typically brewed using lower water temperatures than black tea and is best enjoyed in a delicate and gentle manner to appreciate its unique flavor profile.

Yellow tea is a rare and prized type of tea that is less well-known compared to green, black, and oolong teas. It shares some similarities with green tea but undergoes an additional step in its processing that gives it a unique flavor and appearance.

Iced Tea

Iced tea is a refreshing and popular beverage enjoyed worldwide, especially during hot weather or as a cooling thirst-quencher. It is a chilled version of traditional tea that is served over ice and often sweetened or flavored to suit individual preferences.

We have:

1. Sweetened Iced Tea: Sweetened iced tea is brewed with black tea and sweetened with sugar.

2. Unsweetened Iced Tea: For a lighter and less sweet version, iced tea can be served without any added sweeteners.

3. Iced Green Tea: Using green tea instead of black tea offers a milder and more delicate flavor.

4. Fruity Iced Tea: By infusing the tea with fresh fruit or fruit-flavored syrups, we can create delicious and fruity variations of iced tea.

5. Herbal Iced Tea: Herbal teas like chamomile, peppermint, or hibiscus can also be brewed and served as refreshing iced teas.

Rs. 50 (250mL)
